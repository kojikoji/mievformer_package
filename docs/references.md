## Related Work

### Spatial Transcriptomics Methods

- **Banksy**: A method for identifying spatial domains in spatial transcriptomics data.
- **NicheCompass**: A tool for identifying cell type niches in spatial omics data.
- **GraphST**: Graph neural network-based spatial transcriptomics analysis.
- **STAGATE**: Spatial-temporal graph attention network for spatial transcriptomics.

### Deep Learning Frameworks

- **PyTorch**: Paszke, A., et al. (2019). PyTorch: An Imperative Style, High-Performance Deep Learning Library. NeurIPS.
- **PyTorch Lightning**: Falcon, W., et al. (2019). PyTorch Lightning.

### Single-Cell Analysis

- **AnnData**: Virshup, I., et al. (2021). AnnData: Annotated data.
